About Us

Welcome to the Banaras Anime Club, your home for celebrating anime in Varanasi! Our mission is to create an inclusive community where fans can share their passion and enjoy the unique artistry of anime. We offer a variety of activities, including screenings, discussions, cosplay events, and workshops, to enhance your experience, whether you're a longtime fan or new to the genre. Join us as we connect through our love for anime, support local talent, and create lasting memories in a welcoming environment!
